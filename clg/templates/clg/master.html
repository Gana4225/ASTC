Avanthi's St. Theresa College, established in Garividi, Vizianagaram, is a premier institution committed to providing quality education to students. The college has a strong academic foundation, modern infrastructure, and a vibrant campus life. Over the years, it has become a beacon of knowledge and professional excellence, shaping thousands of students into skilled professionals and responsible citizens.

The institution is well-known for its commitment to academic rigor, research, and extracurricular excellence. The faculty members at Avanthi's St. Theresa College are highly qualified and dedicated to nurturing young minds with a holistic approach. The college provides a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs, ensuring that students get exposure to diverse fields of study.
