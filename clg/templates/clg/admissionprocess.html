Avanthi's St. Theressa Institute of Engineering & Technology (ASTC)

Affiliated with JNTU-GV | EAMCET Code: ASTC

Admission Procedure

Admissions at ASTC follow a structured process to ensure quality education and the best talent intake.

  • Undergraduate (B.Tech): Candidates must have completed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM) with a minimum of 45% marks. Admission is through AP EAMCET counseling.
  • Postgraduate (M.Tech): A relevant B.Tech degree with qualifying marks in GATE or AP PGECET is required for admission.
  • Diploma Courses: Candidates must have completed 10th standard, and admission is based on AP POLYCET rank.
  • Management Quota: A limited number of seats are available for direct admission based on academic performance.
