College Amenities

Academic Facilities

Our college offers a state-of-the-art library with thousands of books, e-books, and research papers to help students excel in their academics. Smart classrooms equipped with projectors and digital boards make learning interactive. Advanced science, engineering, and computer labs ensure hands-on learning experiences.

Student Support Services

The college provides well-maintained hostels with modern amenities, ensuring a comfortable stay for students. The cafeteria offers a variety of nutritious meals at affordable prices. A dedicated medical center is available on campus with emergency care services, and professional counselors provide mental health support.

Sports & Recreation

Students can engage in a wide range of sports, including football, basketball, cricket, badminton, and more. The campus has a fully-equipped gym, jogging tracks, and yoga centers to promote a healthy lifestyle. Recreational zones with gaming facilities provide a great place for relaxation.

Technology & Digital Services

Our campus is Wi-Fi enabled, allowing students to access high-speed internet anywhere. The e-learning portal provides online courses, assignments, and exam materials. High-tech AI and coding labs help students stay updated with the latest technological trends.

Transport & Security

College transport services ensure a safe and convenient commute for students and staff, covering multiple routes. The campus is secured with 24/7 CCTV surveillance and security personnel to ensure safety for all.

Community Spaces

The college has modern auditoriums and seminar halls for academic and cultural events. Student clubs cater to various interests, including technology, arts, music, and literature. Green spaces and eco-friendly initiatives promote sustainability and a clean environment.
