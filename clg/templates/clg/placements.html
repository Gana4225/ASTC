About Avanthi's St. Theresa College, Garividi

Established with a vision of academic excellence, Avanthi's St. Theresa College, located in Garividi, is one of the most prestigious institutions in the region. Our college is dedicated to providing quality education, fostering innovation, and shaping the careers of young minds.
