About Us

Avanthi's St. Theressa Institute

Avanthi's St. Theressa Institute of Engineering and Technology, established in 2001, is a private engineering college located in Garividi, Vizianagaram District, Andhra Pradesh. The institute is spread over 26 acres with a built-up area of 80,000 square feet, offering a conducive environment for academic excellence.

Academic Programs

The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines, including:

  • Computer Science Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Electronics & Communication Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Power Engineering
  • VLSI Design

These programs are approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), ensuring adherence to quality standards in technical education.

Facilities and Infrastructure

  • State-of-the-art laboratories for practical learning
  • Well-equipped library with digital resources
  • Spacious and well-ventilated classrooms
  • Advanced computing center with high-speed internet
  • Sports complex and recreational areas
  • Separate hostel facilities for boys and girls
  • Transportation facilities for students and staff
  • Cafeteria serving hygienic food

Faculty and Research

The faculty members are highly experienced and dedicated to providing quality education. Research and development are actively encouraged, with various funded projects and collaborations with reputed industries and institutions.

Placement and Career Support

The institute has a dedicated placement cell that provides career guidance and training. Top recruiters include TCS, Infosys, Wipro, and other leading companies. Students undergo soft skills training, aptitude tests, and mock interviews to enhance their employability.
