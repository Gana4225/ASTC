Activities

Expert Talk

The Department of ECE at MVGR College of Engineering, Vizianagaram, is proud to organize an Expert Talk on Industrial IoT 4.0, featuring Dr. M. SATYANARAYANA Garu, a distinguished professor in the Department of ECE. This insightful session aims to provide students and faculty with a deeper understanding of the advancements in Industrial IoT 4.0, highlighting its impact on automation, smart industries, and future technological innovations. Dr. Satyanarayana’s expertise will offer valuable insights into the latest developments in IoT-driven solutions, real-world applications, and the transformative role of connected technologies in modern industries. The session will be an excellent opportunity for students to enhance their knowledge, interact with an industry expert, and gain practical insights that will help them stay ahead in the rapidly evolving technological landscape. The talk will also encourage discussions on how IoT is revolutionizing industries by enabling smarter automation, efficient data management, and real-time decision-making. Attendees will benefit from expert guidance on industry trends, career prospects, and the growing significance of IoT in shaping the future of engineering. Scheduled for December 24, 2024, this event promises to be an engaging and enlightening experience, fostering innovation, curiosity, and a deeper appreciation for the role of Industrial IoT in the modern world.

Industry Visit

The Department of Mechanical Engineering at Avanthi’s College, Garividi, successfully organized an enriching industrial visit to the APSRTC Zonal Workshop in Vizianagaram. This visit provided students with a valuable opportunity to gain firsthand knowledge about the functioning, maintenance, and operations of heavy vehicles, enhancing their practical understanding of mechanical engineering concepts. During the visit, students explored various sections of the workshop, including engine overhauling, chassis maintenance, transmission systems, and body fabrication units. Experienced engineers and technicians guided the students through the intricate processes involved in repairing and maintaining APSRTC buses, offering insights into real-world engineering applications. The interactive sessions allowed students to understand the importance of preventive maintenance, efficient resource utilization, and modern advancements in the field of automotive engineering. This industrial visit served as an eye-opener for aspiring mechanical engineers, bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and industrial practices. It also provided an excellent platform for students to interact with industry professionals, gain career insights, and develop a deeper appreciation for the role of mechanical engineering in the transportation sector. Such initiatives by the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Avanthi’s College reinforce the commitment to providing students with practical exposure and industry-relevant learning experiences.
